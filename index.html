Distribution of public/private universities



From the pie chart on the left above, it can be found that most public universities are in South Atlantic, Mid-Atlantic and Oacific regions. The pie chart on the right shows that most private universities are concentrated in South Atlantic, Mid-Atlantic and East North Central.
Through comparison, it can be found that the proportion of private universities in New England and East North Central is much higher than that of public universities, while the proportion of public universities in Mountain and Oacific is higher than that of private universities.

Average Alumni Salary



Overall, the per capita salary of American university alumni is not much different. Among them, the salary per capita of university alumni in New England is the highest, while East South Central is the lowest.
